Our Mission

ART Association is a community-driven organization that brings people together through cultural festivals, art workshops, community gatherings, and creative events. Our goal is to celebrate traditions, support local artists, and create welcoming spaces where families and neighbors can connect, learn, and enjoy cultural experiences together.
